About Us

We Will Make Your Dream True

Vaastu Design & Construction, founded in 2022, is a versatile general contracting company providing a wide range of construction services, including house plan, civil construction, interior design, and more. Our portfolio encompasses diverse projects in industrial, manufacturing, office, retail, recreational, healthcare, and commercial sectors across Pan India.

Our comprehensive suite of services encompasses every facet of your project, from substructure-Civil work to Fabrication, PEB (Pre-Engineered Building), Facade, and Roofing works. With a commitment to precision and quality, we bring together expertise and innovation to create robust structures that stand the test of time.

Vaastu Design & Construction is a leading firm specializing in innovative and sustainable construction solutions. With a commitment to excellence, we offer a comprehensive range of services including modular house plans, advanced 3D designs, and expert interior and exterior design. Our team of skilled professionals ensures that every project meets the highest standards of quality and efficiency. At Vaastu Design & Construction, we blend traditional principles with modern techniques to create spaces that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also environmentally friendly. Trust us to bring your vision to life with precision, creativity, and dedication.

Your trusted partner in delivering excellence for medium and large-scale projects involving steel and concrete structures.
